Book 2: Earth

Characters

Toph Beifong

A blind earthbending prodigy who becomes Aang's earthbending teacher. She is fiercely independent and a master of seismic sense, allowing her to "see" through vibrations in the earth.

Azula

The ruthless and cunning princess of the Fire Nation, sister to Zuko. She is a highly skilled firebender with the rare ability to generate blue fire and lightning.

Mai

One of Azula's childhood friends and a skilled fighter who uses throwing knives and shuriken. She joins Azula in her quest to capture Aang and Zuko.

Ty Lee

Another of Azula's childhood friends, a cheerful and agile acrobat who uses chi-blocking techniques to incapacitate her opponents. She also joins Azula on her missions.

Long Feng

The manipulative and power-hungry leader of the Dai Li, the secret police of Ba Sing Se. He controls the city from behind the scenes and works to maintain the status quo.

Guru Pathik

A wise and spiritual guru who helps Aang understand and unlock the Avatar State by teaching him about the seven chakras and guiding him through the process of spiritual enlightenment.

Episodes

1 - The Avatar State

Aang struggles with the immense power and uncontrollability of the Avatar State. General Fong of the Earth Kingdom tries to trigger the Avatar State to use Aang as a weapon against the Fire Nation, but Aang realizes he must control it himself. Meanwhile, Zuko and Iroh become fugitives after Zuko's sister, Azula, is sent to capture them

2 - The Cave of Two Lovers

On their way to Omashu, the group encounters a nomadic tribe who leads them through a labyrinthine cave system. The group learns the legend of the Cave of Two Lovers and discovers that only by trusting in love can they find their way out. Meanwhile, Zuko and Iroh struggle to adapt to life as fugitives.

3 - Return to Omashu

The group returns to Omashu to find it taken over by the Fire Nation. They meet King Bumi, who has allowed himself to be captured to await the right moment to fight back. Aang and his friends help the citizens escape, and Bumi advises Aang to find an earthbending teacher who waits and listens before striking.

4 - The Swamp

Aang, Katara, and Sokka are drawn to a mysterious swamp where they experience strange visions. They meet swampbenders who explain that the swamp is a living entity. Aang sees a vision of a flying boar, hinting at his future earthbending teacher. Meanwhile, Zuko and Iroh encounter hardships as they try to survive in the Earth Kingdom.

5 - Avatar Day

The group arrives at a town that celebrates "Avatar Day" by burning effigies of past Avatars, holding Aang responsible for a crime committed by his past incarnation, Avatar Kyoshi. Aang clears Kyoshi's name and turns the town's hatred into admiration by saving them from a Fire Nation attack.

6 - The Blind Bandit

Aang searches for an earthbending teacher and meets Toph, a blind but extraordinarily skilled earthbender who competes in underground earthbending tournaments. Despite her parents' overprotectiveness and initial reluctance, Toph joins Aang and his friends as his earthbending teacher.
